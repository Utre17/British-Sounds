The "Tongue Between Teeth" Technique

Fixes: think ‚Üí fink confusion
‚óã

  1. Stick your tongue out just between your teeth
  2. Blow air gently - you should feel air flowing over your tongue
  3. Keep it voiceless - no vibration in your throat
  4. Pull tongue back slightly while maintaining contact

The "Teeth-Lip vs Lip-Round" Method

Fixes: vest/west, vine/wine confusion
‚óã

üÖ¶ W SOUND

  2. Add voice (throat vibrates)
  3. Feel the buzzing sensation

  2. Keep teeth completely away
  3. Glide into the vowel
